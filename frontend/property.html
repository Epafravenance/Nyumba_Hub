About This Property

This stunning ocean-view apartment sits on the 8th floor of one of Masaki's most prestigious residential buildings, offering uninterrupted panoramic views of the Indian Ocean. Designed with a contemporary open-plan layout, the apartment features floor-to-ceiling windows that flood every room with natural light.

The fully-fitted kitchen comes equipped with modern appliances and generous storage. The master bedroom includes a walk-in wardrobe and an en-suite bathroom with premium fixtures. Two additional bedrooms are spacious and well-ventilated, ideal for family or a home office setup.

The building offers 24-hour security, a rooftop swimming pool, a gym, and two dedicated parking bays.
